# The night the arc opened — a riff becomes a roadmap

**Date:** 2026-07-24 · **PRs:** #195 (design, Myk's P6), #196 (T72, stacked)

Myk asked for the open questions in chat instead of a review cycle, and the conversation did more
than answer them — it reshaped the arc's center of gravity.

**Decided in the session:** the §27.8 manifest (all five shapes); §28's six recommendations, now
DECIDED with his word; T72's drop/detach split ("discard means discard," plus the temporary
quarantine). And the reframe that dissolved most of T31: **install is containment; blessing is
adoption.** A loaded module runs fully behind glass — the glass was never darkness; a container
serves its whole door set from its own mount — and blessing law into the root is the separate,
rarer, deliberate act.

**Then the riff went somewhere better.** Myk: this tool doesn't take off without discovery — a
hub where you find the highest-rated Post schema and pull it in, where an app's manifest declares
it mirrors that schema so compatibility is knowable up front. The machinery nearly gives it away:
a hub is a Loam store whose ground is schemas and ratings; and the mirroring claim is verifiable
by ADDRESS EQUALITY at the schema-identity level, which survives adoption re-signing —
**compatibility as arithmetic, not marketing.** From there: compositional content addresses (the
movie-night assembly — rung 3's named consumer finally arrived), and bridges — two Posts either
provably identical despite divergent provenance, or bridged into functional interchangeability by
adapter modules across three axes: vocabulary (a reading over both shapes), semantics (derived
functions with replay-verifiable provenance), identity (same-as claims, signed and strikeable).
The compat relation becomes a graph; reachable means interchangeable; the bridge itself is a
module with an address. His close: "It's all just legos man."

**T31's spec then earned its criteria the hard way**: an independent premortem (six findings —
the headline: an unguarded adoption would silently capture the root's living `Post` lens) and
four adversarial-review rounds, re-run after every fold. Round 1 refused it outright; round 2
caught the fix's own wound (a CAS with no named mechanism is a wish — the single-writer gateway
door is the mechanism); round 4 arrived at two definitional MEDIUMs. Seven criteria became
twenty-two.

**T72 ran the same gauntlet as a build.** The lens panel found the zero-ids retry (a partial
purge empties the READABLE surface, and the old guard skipped the verdict exactly there), the §25
pen (rows a read sets aside are invisible to id-keyed purge), and the sharp one — **an erasure
issued during a detach window arrived at reattach as data and executed as nothing**, the
forgotten byte resolving live beside its own tombstone. The fix settles erasure debt before the
pool's reader exists, for every open. Four new tickets (T76–T79) stock the arc: the Merkle-set,
the hub with bridges, dynamic mounts, and the renderer's artifact target.

The standing lesson, again and in one sentence: every real defect tonight was found by a reader
who did not share the author's premise.
